Sripalee college was started on 20th May in 1934.The Foundation stone was laid by Rabindranath Thagor.The founder of the school was Mr.Wilmet A Perera.The term of Sripalee means "The Place where the Indian goddess Lakshmi lives". She is the goddess of aesthetics & prosperity. Sripalee college is situated at the base of "Kapukula" hill. It is a duplicate institute similar to "Shanthi Nikethan" of India .Our Alma mater presents all rounders to the Srilankan society
